Our Story


Livingston Ever After specializes in all things personalized. A small business run by a Bonus Mom/Daughter duo, we are sure we can take your vision and make it come to life.

Shelbie Livingston got her love of crafts from her paternal grandmother and is never afraid to try new things. While her first passion is education, Shelbie often finds that the two worlds are constantly intertwined. Shelbie can take her love for crafts and make lessons come alive. When she is crafting, she is able to use her teacher's brain and attention to detail to make a customer's vision a reality.

Zion Livingston has always enjoyed watching her mom and stepmom work at crafts and has always wanted to be involved in the crafting projects. When she is not at school or dance practice, you can find her eager to help with crafts and add her personal touch to anything.

This duo is ready for anything. If you see something not listed, feel free to send us a message. IF we can't deliver, we can recommend another small business that can.
